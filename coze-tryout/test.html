Reputable Insurer - QA / UAT Tester

Gravitas Recruitment Group (Hong Kong) Limited
View all jobs
Central, Central and Western District
Corporate Finance & Investment Banking (Banking & Financial Services)
Contract/Temp
$25,000 – $30,000 per month
Posted 5d ago
Quick apply

Responsibilities:

  • Collaborate with project stakeholders, business analysts, and developers to understand software requirements and user stories.
  • Design and execute UAT test cases based on functional specifications and business requirements.
  • Identify, document, and report defects, ensuring their resolution and retesting until closure.
  • Conduct end-to-end testing to validate system behaviour
  • Assist in the creation and maintenance of testing documentation, including test plans, test scripts, and test data.
  • Work closely with business users to gain insights into their needs, providing recommendations for quality improvement.
  • Participate in team meetings and provide input on process enhancements and best practices.

Qualifications:

  • Minimum 1 year experience in software testing and quality assurance
  • Familiarity with software testing methodologies and tools.
  • Basic understanding of insurance industry concepts and processes is a plus.
  • Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with excellent attention to detail.
  • Effective communication skills, both written and verbal, to collaborate with diverse stakeholders.
  • Proactive attitude and willingness to learn and grow in a fast-paced, dynamic environment.

Employer questions

Your application will include the following questions:
  • Which of the following statements best describes your right to work in Hong Kong?
  • What's your expected monthly basic salary?
  • How many years' experience do you have as a User Acceptance Tester?
