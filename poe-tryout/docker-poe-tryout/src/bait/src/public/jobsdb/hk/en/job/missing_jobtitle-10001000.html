Job Highlights

Job Description

What will you do as a Software Automation QA Engineer

You are responsible for: 

  • automation testing of applications on mobile, tablet, web platforms.
  • ensuring the quality of media apps being used for millions of users globally
  • work closely with product and development teams

What is offered to you?

In addition to a monthly salary of up to 40,000 HKD, the following is offered;

  • 16 Annual leave and birthday leave
  • Medical and dental insurance coverage
  • Global exposure
  • Discretionary bonus

What is expected of you?

  • BS degree in Computer Science, Engineering or a related subject with at least 1 year QA automation experience.
  • Knowledge about how to dev on Linux.
  • Java/Python/NodeJS.
  • Knowledge about version control.
  • Exp in CI/CD practice.
  • Exp about FE test automation, knowledge about selenium/appium.
  • Exp about BE test automation, knowledge about postman/rest-assured.
  • Exp about BDD:cucumber or TDD: Robot framework.
  • Knowledge about Docker.
  • Knowledge about Jenkins.[LB1] 
  • Exp about test reporting framework.
  • Exp about device fram.
  • Exp about test case management tools.
  • Good command in speaking and writing in Cantonese, Mandarin and English

Where are you going to work?

We are looking for an experienced Software Automation QA Engineer to devote your career with extensive exposure in a leading company. The team collaborates together closely to ensure smooth ongoing and success for various projects. You will be given high independence at work to bring out your best capabilities while being innovative.
